If you have a Shopify store, you can integrate with PayMongo with no coding required! All you'll need is an activated PayMongo account to get started.

* If you don't have a PayMongo account, you can sign up [here](https://dashboard.paymongo.com/signup)
* If you have an account but haven't activated it yet, activate your account [here](https://dashboard.paymongo.com/activate)

### Installing PayMongo on Shopify

**Once activated, follow these steps to integrate the PayMongo plugin into your Shopify store:**

1. Go to your store's Payment settings and click on the **Add payment methods** button.

![](/images/c9954c8-Screen_Shot_2022-07-14_at_9.39.06_AM.png "Screen Shot 2022-07-14 at 9.39.06 AM.png")

1. Click on the **Search by provider** option and search for **_Secure Payments via PayMongo_**. Alternatively, you could also access the plugin through this [link](https://accounts.shopify.com/store-login?redirect=settings%2Fpayments%2Falternative-providers%2F1059415). Click the **Activate** button.

![](/images/3ebe6bd-Screen_Shot_2022-07-14_at_9.39.21_AM.png "Screen Shot 2022-07-14 at 9.39.21 AM.png")

1. Upon clicking, you'll be redirected to the plugin's connection page. Click on the **Connect** button at the bottom portion of the connection page.

![](/images/3b3918a-Screen_Shot_2022-07-14_at_9.46.30_AM.png "Screen Shot 2022-07-14 at 9.46.30 AM.png")

1. Upon clicking, you'll be redirected to the installation page. Click on the **Install app** button located on the top right portion of the page.
2. Upon clicking, you'll be redirected to the plugin's PayMongo account connection page. Input your PayMongo **public key** and **secret key**, and click on the **Connect** button. You can find these on the [Developer's tab](https://dashboard.paymongo.com/developers) of your PayMongo dashboard.

![](/images/bd6aa2b-Screen_Shot_2022-08-04_at_9.24.07_AM.png "Screen Shot 2022-08-04 at 9.24.07 AM.png")

1. Upon entering the keys, you'll be greeted with the screen below. Click on the **Go Back to Shopify** button, and you're done! You should now be able to accept payments via PayMongo on your Shopify store.

![](/images/03c417c-Screen_Shot_2022-08-04_at_9.34.16_AM.png "Screen Shot 2022-08-04 at 9.34.16 AM.png")
